About Me

Hi, I’m Vikram Aaditya, and I’ve been passionate about self-improvement for the past 4 years. Over this time, I’ve gained a wide range of knowledge about facial aesthetics and what makes someone visually appealing—both from a mathematical and subjective perspective. I’ve analyzed and rated over 2,000 faces, which has given me unique insights into patterns of attractiveness and how small changes can make a big difference. My goal is to help people understand their own potential and improve their appearance in a realistic and effective way. With 45,000+ subscribers on my YouTube channel, I’ve built a community that’s all about understanding and enhancing facial aesthetics. Whether you’re looking to improve your look or just curious about what makes a face attractive, I’m here to share what I’ve learned and help you on your journey. Let’s grow and transform together!
